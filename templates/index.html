GARB RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM

With the development of e-commerce technology, a growing number of people prefer to purchase clothes on the e-commerce websites. Therefore, an effective recommendation system is necessary for customers and we believe that our Garb Recommendation System can prove to be one. In this we have used euclidean distance between the titles of the Apparel to recommend related item to the user. The time taken to filter through the titles would also be very less in this case which would henceforth solve the problem of the user to a greater extent. However, we believe that a lot more advancement could be done to our algorithm and we will look forward to do the same in future.
